* NAME
tictactoe - play tic-tac-toe against the computer

* SYNOPSIS
play tictactoe

* DESCRIPTION
A classic 3x3 tic-tac-toe game where you play X against a basic AI opponent.
Click any empty cell to make a move. Press R to reset the board.

* CONTROLS
- Click: place your mark
- R: reset the board
- ESC: return to terminal
